Net-probe bench · arch-sweep prestudy · held-out verdict · 2026-07-18

Capacity is not the lever.

Held-out replication (seed 1,000,000, outside every training stream) of the NN-utilization pilot: b0k seeds s0/s1/s2 probed on a netdmcts:1,320 teacher set (9,269 states) and an mcts:100 companion set (33,186 states), plus the flat-tower arm. The picture is stable across all three seeds and both teachers: the 64-wide towers are nowhere near full; what limits them is conditioning and content, not size.

Not met — width/depth arms dead C1 · Capacity-bound? PR/width > 0.5, or heavy saturation with high PR Max observed PR/width is 0.28 (pi_a1). Value towers collapse to PR 2–5 of 64. Saturation only ever co-occurs with low PR.
Not met — best is +0.008, noise C2 · Trunk computes features the head wastes? Any layer beats raw obs by ≥ +0.03 on action decoding No layer, no seed, no teacher. Every tower layer loses tactical information relative to a linear probe on the raw observation.
Met — the surviving arms C3 · Low PR + flat probes across depth ⇒ sweep targets conditioning and the head First-layer tanh saturation up to 94% with unnormalized inputs: obs normalization / LayerNorm and head-objective work are the only justified arms. Play-time search (rbeam RoR) remains the lever.

Data. Teacher-vs-baseline games recorded at seed 1,000,000 (outside every training stream). netdmcts set: teacher netdmcts:1,320,1.5,depot:shared/shared_s0.zip,depot:ldraft/ldraft_s0.zip (the promoted E23 search recipe), 4 opponent shards × 50 games × 2 seats = 400 games, 9,269 battle decisions, shards merged with game_id offsets. Companion sets: mcts:100 teacher, 150 games, 33,186 decisions each in token and flat encodings.

Probes. Ridge regression (l2 = 1.0) on standardized activations, game-level 80/20 split. Action probe is masked multinomial over 155 logits; outcome probe is win/loss AUC. Raw-observation probes are the control that separates “the layer computes” from “the layer re-embeds the input”. Probe deltas < 0.01 are noise per the seed spread.

Caveats. Outcome AUC on the netdmcts set is uninformative (the teacher wins ~everything; majority baseline 0.996). netdmcts states are also easier for the action probe (masked-majority 0.490 vs 0.311), and b0k's higher argmax agreement there (0.52–0.55 vs 0.36–0.37 on mcts sets) partly reflects the shared-family critic guiding that search. Re-init clones use PyTorch default init, not SB3 orthogonal — read init markers coarsely.
